SRI LANKAN TURTLES

Sri Lankan Turtles

Sri lanka is home to five species of sea turtles,including the endangered leatherback,green,olive ridley,loggerhead,and hawks.There are three species of freshwater turtles and one terrestrial tortoise in sri lanka.The freshwater turtles are Parker's Black Turtle(Melanochelys trijuga pakeri)Sri Lanka Black Turtle(Melanchelys trijuge thermalis) and the soft or Flapshell turtle.Turtles Foods:Animal based food sources for turtles can include processed pet foods like drained sardines, turtle pellets, and trout chow.
