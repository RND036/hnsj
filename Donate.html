"Saving Sea Turtles and Eggs from Poachers"



Sea Turtles are global, migrating thousands of miles to beaches around the world to lay their eggs. We, the people who care about their survival are part of an intimate global connection! Even with the catastrophic pandemic of Covid19 slamming our countries, our organization is humbled by concerned people around the world donating to save our vulnerable marine animals. We welcome any size donation to support the turtle protectors who patrol the beach at night rescuing sea turtles and their eggs from poachers.
